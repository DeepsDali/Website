My Interests

As an astronomy enthusiast, I was excited with the much awaited launch of the James Webb Telescope. Why is it so special? The telescope uses infra red light to “see” all the way back to the first generation of stars created 13.5 billion years ago, just 300 million years after the Big Bang.


Check out my slider below to see some of my favourite images taken by the telescope this year. You can play and pause the slider or use the left and right arrows to browse at your pace. You can even try uploading any of your favourite images to my collection using the upload button.
